## Conventions (compact rules)

- **Location**: in this dedicated skills repo, skills go in `skills/<name>/` at the root (versioned, travels with the clone). Inside an app project, the equivalent layout is `.agents/skills/`. Personal/global skills stay in `~/.claude/skills/` and are NOT committed here.
- **Author**: `KritiusOne` in SKILL.md frontmatter. Do NOT use the skill-creator default `gentleman-programming`.
- **allowed-tools**: declare in frontmatter to pre-authorize binaries (e.g. `Bash(playwright-cli:*)`).
- **Docs**: document new custom skills in `AGENTS.md` under "Herramientas de AI custom".
